About Hogwarts University

The Legendary Castle

Hogwarts University, established in the highlands of Scotland, stands as a beacon of magical education for centuries. The castle, known as the Heart of Wizardry, has been enchanted with spells of protection, illusion, and secret passageways known only to the wise.

Founded by the four greatest witches and wizards of their time—Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin—Hogwarts remains a symbol of unity, bravery, and magic.

Over the years, it has witnessed battles, secrets, legends, and heroes—none greater than Harry Potter, who once walked these enchanted corridors and stood up to the darkest wizard of all time.

From the Great Hall’s floating candles to the hidden common rooms, from the Forbidden Forest to the Room of Requirement—every corner of Hogwarts pulses with mystery and majesty.

Today, Hogwarts University opens its gates not just to British witches and wizards, but to young sorcerers from around the magical globe. Here, knowledge isn’t just taught—it’s cast, conjured, and brewed.
